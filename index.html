About

Built at the intersection
of capital & climate

I am a 24-year-old Investment Analyst at Climate Collective Foundation, working at the frontier of climate tech investing. My background in chemical engineering from IIPE Visakhapatnam (JEE Advanced admit) grounds me in first-principles thinking... which I apply to evaluating climate ventures, carbon offset projects, and sustainable finance instruments.

I have evaluated 1,000+ climate tech startups across carbon markets, circular economy, and clean energy; facilitated 5 deals; and led 30+ founder–investor introductions. Before this I ran a university incubation programme at The Sustainability Mafia's SusVentures division, supporting 20+ pre-incubated climate startups, and co-authored the India Climate Tech Opportunity Map.

Earlier I served as Chief of Staff at Longstraw Carbon, a carbon market startup in Delhi, where I led commercial integration of offset projects, business development, and project assessment. I am drawn to roles close to founders and capital allocators... building toward a career at the intersection of entrepreneurship, deep-tech climate, and systemic capital deployment.
